How to Make Tacos!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatos
  • Lettuce
  • Taco Seasonings
  • Taco Shells
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese

Process

  1. Cut up the lettuce and the tomatoes.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan, and add in the taco seasoning.
  3. Put the desired amount of ground beef into a taco shell.
  4. Next, pick from the condiments that you have assembled and put those also in the taco shell.
  5. Eat the taco.
